Small Spanish sandwiches

Description

Everyone will like small Spanish sandwiches. The snack is very popular among the local population, as it is easy to prepare and eat with great pleasure!

Ingredients

  • sheep cheese- 4 teaspoons
  • egg- 1 piece
  • salami sausage- To taste
  • peanuts- 15 pieces

Steps for cooking

  1. First, boil a hard-boiled egg.
  2. In a blender, grind the boiled egg, sheep cheese, and a handful of peanuts. Put the resulting mass on a piece of salami and roll it up.
  3. You can also cook baguette slices in a toaster. Enjoy!
