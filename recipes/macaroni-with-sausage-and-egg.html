Macaroni with sausage and egg

Description

Ease of preparation, excellent taste and minimal ingredients are the main characteristics of this wonderful dish. I cook it for breakfast or lunch, and I suggest you try it too.

Ingredients

  • pasta - 400 grams
  • sausages - 3 pieces
  • milk - 1 glass
  • egg - 1 piece
  • mustard powder - 1 teaspoon
  • salt - To taste

Steps for cooking

  1. First of all, of course, we should boil the pasta in lightly salted water until it is fully cooked and drain it.
  2. While the pasta is cooking, you can grate the cheese on a coarse grater, cut the sausages into slices and beat the egg in a bowl.
  3. Add the beaten egg, a glass of milk, cheese, mustard powder and sliced sausages to the hot pasta and mix well.
  4. Put the resulting pasta mass into a baking dish and put it in a preheated 180-degree oven for about 15 minutes.
